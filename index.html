Space

The final frontier


You are in command of the USS Enterprise. After leaving Betazed, you have been tasked

with ensuring that the dignitary from Andros III is safely ferried from the gathering

of the Council of Planets to a remote station located in the Alpha Quadrant.

Only then will you be able to travel through the Bajoran Wormhole to the Gamma Quadrant.


Navigate the quadrant, protect the dignitary and defend the Enterprise against any attacks.

Good Luck!
